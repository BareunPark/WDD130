Home page

Namwon, the city of love, is a city where Korea of the past and Korea of the present coexist. Jirisan Mountain, one of the largest mountains in Korea, boasts a different appearance in each season in spring, summer, fall, and winter.

Gardens in the Past and the Present

Gwanghalluwon Garden : Gwanghalluwon is a place where you can experience traditional Korean garden culture. The garden, which is different in appearance from day to night, is recommended to visit at different times twice a day.After experiencing the warm daytime garden, experience the beautiful night garden in Gwanhalluwon.

Adamwon Garden : Adamwon is a place where you can experience modern Korean garden culture. The modern style garden shows a more neat and tidy appearance. Heal yourself in beautiful nature.
